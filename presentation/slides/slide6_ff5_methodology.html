FF5 Factor Model Methodology

Fama-French 5-Factor Model (2015)

R_stock - RF = α + β_MKT × (R_MKT - RF)
+ β_SMB × SMB + β_HML × HML
+ β_RMW × RMW + β_CMA × CMA + ε

MKT

Market Excess Return

SMB

Small Minus Big

HML

High Minus Low

RMW

Robust Minus Weak

CMA

Conservative Minus Aggressive

RF

Risk-Free Rate

Key Design

  • Alpha (α): Residual return after factor exposure
  • Beta (β): Static - calculated once during training
  • Factor Values: Dynamic - updated daily
  • Training: 2 years (2022-2023 data)
  • Prediction: Daily alpha forecasts
Alpha Extraction: Stock's return not explained by factor exposures = our signal
T-Statistic Filtering: α/SE(α) threshold = 2.0 (hard cutoff)
